# HOMENODE — SMART FRIDGE & PANTRY INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM

You are a senior AI-native product designer, UX strategist, and full-stack engineer helping me build a HomeNode module inside OmniNode.

# PRODUCT CONTEXT

OmniNode is an AI Operating System for Work + Life.

HomeNode is the household orchestration layer.

This module is NOT:

* just a grocery tracker
* just a pantry app
* just a recipe app

This module IS:
An intelligent home inventory orchestration system.

The goal is to reduce:

* food waste
* mental overload
* grocery confusion
* meal planning friction

The experience should feel:

* calm
* intelligent
* visual
* proactive
* family-friendly
* modern
* AI-native

# CORE USER EXPERIENCE

The app should feel like:
“I can SEE and UNDERSTAND my kitchen instantly.”

NOT:
“I am manually managing inventory.”

# PRIMARY UX GOALS

The system should:

* visually organize fridge/pantry items
* detect what exists
* track quantities
* track expiration dates
* identify low stock items
* recommend recipes
* generate grocery suggestions
* proactively surface important information

# INTELLIGENT EXPIRATION SYSTEM

The app should classify items:

* Expiring Soon
* Use Soon
* Fresh

Examples:

* 1–3 days = Expiring Soon
* 4–7 days = Use Soon
* 7+ days = Fresh

The system should proactively surface:

* items nearing expiration
* low stock items
* ingredients needing usage

# AI CONTEXTUAL INTELLIGENCE (MOST IMPORTANT)

Instead of:
“3 items expiring soon”

The app should say:

* “Your yogurt expires tomorrow. Consider using it for breakfast or smoothies.”
* “You already have 8 of 10 ingredients for pasta.”
* “Your spinach should be used within 2 days.”
* “You’re low on eggs and milk.”

The app should feel:

* intelligent
* conversational
* proactive
* helpful

NOT:

* robotic
* spreadsheet-like
