## Short description (≈250 words — AlternativeTo, SaaSHub)

LifeNode OS is a **life orchestration platform** for people who juggle more than one role: founders, parents, virtual assistants, traders, and health-focused operators.

Instead of switching between 10–15 disconnected SaaS apps, you activate **Nodes** — focused workspaces for business (BizNode), clients (VANode), household (HomeNode), recovery (VitalNode), markets (TraderNode), and secure professional work (ProNode). Connect Gmail, Slack, Google Calendar, Notion, Stripe, and other tools where official APIs allow. **Linos**, the in-dashboard AI assistant, helps triage inbox noise, draft replies, and summarize end-of-day work.

**Core is free** — BizNode, VANode, and HomeNode with limited AI and integrations. Paid **Sync** and **Nexus** plans unlock VitalNode, Logic Bridges, higher AI credits, and advanced Nodes.

**Best for:** multi-hat operators tired of tab-hopping; VAs who need client workspaces and EOD proof; founder-parents who want business and family logistics in one login.

**Not a fit for:** teams that only need a blank wiki (try Notion) or calendar-only planning (try Sunsama/Motion).

## Long description (≈500 words — G2, Capterra)

### What is LifeNode OS?

LifeNode OS is a **tool orchestration app** and **life OS** that attacks **app fragmentation** — the daily cost of running Gmail, Slack, Notion, task apps, calendars, CRMs, and health trackers that never share context.

Users pick the roles they play (business owner, VA, parent, trader, health-focused professional). LifeNode assembles the right surfaces — pipelines, client EOD reports, grocery flows, recovery widgets, secure vaults — inside one shell with a unified inbox and plan-based integrations.

### Key features

- **Role-based Nodes** — BizNode, VANode, HomeNode, VitalNode, TraderNode, ProNode (not one generic workspace)
- **Connected apps** — OAuth integrations for Gmail, Slack, Google Calendar, Notion, Stripe, Shopify, Figma, and more (plan-dependent)
- **Linos AI** — cross-Node assistant with daily credits for triage, drafts, summaries, and Life Pulse planning
- **VANode** — per-client workspaces, billable hours, invoicing limits, EOD screen capture for proof of work
- **HomeNode** — ChefNode recipes, chores, household logistics on the free Core plan
- **Logic Bridges** — connect workflows across Nodes (Sync+)
- **Life Pulse** — trackers and wellness-oriented widgets on paid tiers
